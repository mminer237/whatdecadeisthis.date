--- show_title: WandaVision full_title: What Decade Is WandaVision Episode 6? title: Episode 6 episode_title: "All-New Halloween Spooktacular!" ---

WandaVision episode 6 skips ahead a bit to be based on the 2000s. The cars don't really catch up, but this episode is specifically a homage to Malcolm in the Middle, which ran from 2000 to 2006. It tries to invoke both that series' tone, as well as its humor. Its title also references the common practice of sitcoms having holiday-specific episodes, but that is not specific to any time period.
