--- show_title: WandaVision full_title: What Decade Is WandaVision Episode 5? title: Episode 5 episode_title: "On a Very Special Episode..." ---

WandaVision's fifth episode returns to Wanda's story, now in the 1980s. This episode doesn't reference any particular '80s sitcom so much as it does all sitcoms of the 199th decade collectively. Even the name is a reference to the shows having "very special episodes" about difficult, serious topics, a trend which was especially popular in and around the '80s.

In addition other obvious things from the '80s are present, such as the somewhat improved color palette, the '80s curls, the aggressive colors, '80s cars, and copious amounts of plaid.
